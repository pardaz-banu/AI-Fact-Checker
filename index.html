AI Fact Checker

Uses the Gemini API with Google Search grounding.

What Claims Does This Tool Check?

This tool uses AI grounded in real-time Google Search results. It works best with **verifiable, factual claims** that can be looked up on the web, such as:

  • **Current Events:** "Who won the latest F1 Grand Prix?"
  • **Historical Data:** "What is the capital of Australia?"
  • **Public Figures/Organizations:** "What is the mission statement of the World Health Organization?"

It is **not** designed for opinions, hypotheticals, or personal questions.
